Company Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
Sick leave is granted for up to 10 days per year with a doctor's note.
Maternity leave is 6 months fully paid. Paternity leave is 15 calendar days.

Remote Work Policy:
Employees can work remotely for up to 3 days per week.
Full remote work requires VP-level approval.
All remote employees must have a stable internet connection and a private workspace.
